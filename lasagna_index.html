01
Ragù
TOTAL · ~3 HOURS

BUILD PHASE

0:00
Start SoffrittoHeat olive oil over medium-low. Add onion, carrot, celery. Sweat 8–10 min. No color.
0:10
Add MeatAdd beef and pork. Break up evenly across the pot.
0:10–0:25
Browning PhaseCRITICALCook until properly browned. No grey meat. Color must develop. This is the flavor.

FINISH PHASE

0:25
DeglazeAdd white wine. Reduce until almost dry, ~5 min. Wine goes BEFORE tomato. Always.
0:30
Add TomatoesHand-crushed pelati. Salt lightly.
0:30–3:30
SimmerLow heat, gentle bubble. Stir every 20–30 min. Final texture: thick, not watery. Minimum 3 hours, no exceptions.

DOUGH + REST

MIX
KneadCombine flours and eggs. Mix and knead 10–12 min until smooth and elastic.
REST
30 Minutes CoveredWrap tightly. Do not skip the rest. The gluten needs it.

ROLL + COOK

ROLL
#7 → #2Start at setting #7, work down to #2. Even thickness throughout the sheet.
BOIL
~1 minuteHeavily salted water — like pasta water. Cook sheets ~1 min.
SHOCK
Ice BathMANDATORYImmediately into ice bath. Lay flat on oiled trays. Do not stack wet.

06
COOL · PORTION
· FREEZE
CRITICAL FOR QUALITY
RUSHING THIS WRECKS THE PAN
CRITICAL
01

Rest

Cool at room temperature 20–30 min. Then transfer to fridge until fully firm. Cutting hot lasagna will collapse the layers.

02

Cut

Once firm, portion into 12 equal pieces. Clean, vertical knife strokes. Wipe blade between cuts for clean lines.

03

Pack + Blast

Transfer each portion to foil containers. Into the blast chiller immediately. Freeze fully before moving to long-term storage.

Besciamella

PASS — Holds shape when cut

FAIL — Falling apart, too much liquid
SOP / 08 · SERVICE

REHEAT
TO dine-in
STANDARD

Frozen portions are only as good as the reheat. Match the dine-in experience every single time. Crust on top, molten center, layers intact. If it doesn't hit, it doesn't go out.

A

From Frozen — Convection Oven

375°F · 190°C35–45 MIN
  1. Keep in foil container. Cover loosely with foil.
  2. Bake covered 25 min. Internal temp climbing.
  3. Remove foil cover. Bake 10–15 min until top recrisps.
  4. Probe center: 165°F / 74°C minimum.
  5. Rest 5 min before plating. Layers set.
B

From Thawed (Refrigerated 24 hr) — Convection

375°F · 190°C20–25 MIN
  1. Cover loosely with foil. Bake 12 min.
  2. Uncover. Bake 8–12 min, top golden, edges bubbling.
  3. Probe center: 165°F / 74°C minimum.
  4. Rest 5 min before plating.
C

Express — Microwave Then Salamander

FOR SPEED ONLY~6 MIN
  1. From thawed: microwave covered, medium power, 3–4 min.
  2. Transfer to oven-safe plate.
  3. Under salamander 60–90 sec to set the crust.
  4. Probe center: 165°F / 74°C minimum.
  5. Use only when ticket time demands it. Method A or B preferred.
D

Plating Standard

EVERY TICKETNO EXCEPTIONS
  1. Warm plate. Center the portion, layers facing diner.
  2. Spoon any released ragù around the base — never on top.
  3. Fresh grated Parmigiano. Single basil leaf optional.
  4. Wipe rim. Run it.
09
MAESTRO'S
NOTES
FABRIZIO'S RULES
OPERATIONALIZED
RULE 01

"Browning the meat is non-negotiable. No color, no flavor."

RULE 02

"Wine before tomato. Always. The acid needs the alcohol gone first."

RULE 03

"Three hours minimum on the ragù. The clock is the recipe."

RULE 04

"Besciamella stays loose. Heavy sauce makes heavy lasagna."

RULE 05

"Ice bath for the pasta. Mandatory. No exceptions."

RULE 06

"Do not rush the cooling. A hot cut is a broken pan."
